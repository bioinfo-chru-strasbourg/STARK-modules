SIFT on dbSNP


SIFT Predictions on Protein IDs: SIFT was applied to all human nonsynonymous SNPs in dbSNP (build 129). The sequence alignment used for prediction were taken from NCBI's precomputed BLAST searches and was composed of either the best hit to other organisms or all BLAST hits. Scoring is as described in Genome Research 12: 436-446.
