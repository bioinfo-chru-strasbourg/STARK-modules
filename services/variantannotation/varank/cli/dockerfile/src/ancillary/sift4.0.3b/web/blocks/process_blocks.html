Blocks Multiple Alignment Processor

Blocks are carved out from a protein multiple sequence alignment to provide input for:
  • Sequence logos
  • Phylogenetic trees
  • Structures
  • Blocks-based searches, which may be performed using search engines such as
    • MAST
    • LAMA
  • CODEHOP oligonucleotide primers
  • SIFT deleterious substitution predictions
Your multiple alignment may include up to 400 sequences in one of the following formats:
  • Blocks format such as is provided by Block Maker.
    • You may format alignments manually as blocks.

  • FASTA, CLUSTAL/STOCKHOLM or MSF format.

Ungapped regions between the requested minimum and maximum widths will be carved out of the submitted multiple alignment and converted to Blocks format. You can insert a column of gaps in your FASTA, CLUSTAL or MSF alignment to force block edges.
